### English
I am a master's student in the Graduate School of Engineering at Kyoto University.  
My main areas are **AI systems, RAG, LLM systems, and distributed systems**, using Python, Rust, and TypeScript.

My earlier work focused on **building production-oriented AI workflows on AWS, implementing and delivering RAG systems, and developing AI avatars**, with an emphasis on turning AI into systems people can actually use.

More recently, I have also been working on **local LLM infrastructure and distributed inference environments using DGX Spark, vLLM, and Ray**.

On the research side, I am also interested in **mathematical modeling and Markov Decision Processes (MDPs)**.
